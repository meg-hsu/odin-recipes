Tomato and Egg Rice

Description

Then look no further than the delightful combination of tomato and egg rice. Tomato and Egg Rice is a staple in many Asian cuisines and is known for its simplicity and robust flavors.

Whether you’re a busy professional, a student with limited cooking skills, or simply craving a homemade comfort meal, Tomato and Egg Rice is a winner. Let’s dive into how to make this easy tomato and egg rice dish that will leave your taste buds dancing!

Ingredients

  • 4 medium Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp of White Pepper
  • 2.5 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1.5 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 green onions (chopped)
  • 3 garlic (chopped)
  • 1 tsp of dashi powder (optional)
  • Salt
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoon cold water
  • 1 bowl of cooked rice

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix eggs, 1 tsp of white pepper, and salt.
  2. Drizzle oil into a pot and turn the heat to medium-high. Drizzle in the egg and set it for 30 seconds before scrambling together. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the tomato, white parts of the green onion, and garlic. Turn the heat to medium-high and saute together for 3-4 minutes or until everything is soft.
  4. Season with ketchup, soy sauce, dashi powder, and white pepper, and mix well.
  5. Add back in the egg, and mix for another 30 seconds.
  6. Mix cornstarch and cold water together, and pour into the pan, mix, and let it simmer for another 2 minutes.
  7. **Depending if you added dashi powder, you can season with more salt and white pepper to taste.
  8. Finish by adding cooked rice and the green onion; enjoy!
